About Me

My name is Jennifer Mazanec. I was born and raised in the Cleveland, Ohio area, although I did spend a few of my early years abroad. I attended the University of Notre Dame (Go Irish!) where I earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology with a minor in Business Economics. I decided to pursue a career in Human Resources working for a retail company in their Learning and Development Department.

I soon realized this career path was not for me and started to think about what really interested and excited me (better late than never). I have always loved and had a knack for technology, and I found that I am most engaged in projects that have a technical component. This along with a desire for a creative aspect in my work prompted me to pursue web development. This brought me to the Case Western Reserve University Coding Boot Camp.

In the rare moments when I am not absorbed in code, I enjoy reading and watching TV (nothing too serious ☺) and playing tennis. I love to travel and explore new places (even in my own backyard). I am an avid BuzzFeed follower and spend far too much time considering what my Chipotle order says about my personality. I also attempt to wrangle my two very unruly dogs.

Bamazon

Bamazon

Project Description:

Bamazon is a Node.js application run in command line which displays a products tables from a MySQL database and prompts the user for what item they would like to purchase, how many, and what their total is based on the item price. The application checks the database to ensure the item number is valid and there is sufficient quantity. The database is updated to reflect the new quantity.

Technology Used:

  • MySQL
  • Node
  • Inquirer Node Module
  • MySQL Node Module
